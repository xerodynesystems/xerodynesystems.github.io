Our Dark Genesis

The idea for Xerodyne Systems wasn't born in a boardroom; it was forged in the trenches. A group of us – Vinnie, Ruben, Sean, and Hunter – were frankly fed up. We were tired of battling insanely costly software that consistently underdelivered, rarely worked as promised, and suffered from an excruciatingly slow feature turnaround.
